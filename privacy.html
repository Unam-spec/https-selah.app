SELAH — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SELAH is a personal vision board application. It connects to Pinterest solely to allow the user to browse and display their own Pinterest boards and pins within the app.

SELAH does not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit any personal data. Pinterest access tokens are stored locally in the user's own browser and are never sent to any third-party server.

This application is for personal use only.
